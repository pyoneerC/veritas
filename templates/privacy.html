Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use Basalt Protocol, we may collect:

  • Files you upload: Images and documents submitted for notarization
  • Cryptographic hashes: SHA-256 fingerprints of your files
  • Blockchain records: Public transaction data on Solana
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about how you use our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide cryptographic signing and notarization services
  • Store your files on IPFS for permanent, decentralized access
  • Create immutable blockchain records of your content
  • Improve and optimize our services

3. Data Storage & Security

Files are stored on IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), a decentralized network. Once uploaded, content is distributed across multiple nodes and cannot be deleted or modified. Blockchain anchors are permanent and publicly visible on the Solana network.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Pinata: IPFS pinning and gateway services
  • Solana: Blockchain network for transaction anchoring
  • DigiCert: Timestamp authority for C2PA signatures

5. Your Rights

Due to the nature of blockchain and IPFS technology, data cannot be deleted once submitted. Please only submit content you have the right to notarize and are comfortable being permanently recorded.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@basaltprotocol.com
